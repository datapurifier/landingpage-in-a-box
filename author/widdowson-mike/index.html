IODP Expedition 397T Color reflectance

Abstract Color reflectance data were measured on section halves using an integration sphere and a UV-VIS spectrophotometer mounted on the Section Half Multisensor Logger (SHMSL). Spectral counts are recorded in the range of 380 to 700 nm, covering the visible spectrum, and binned in ~2 nm bins. Spectral data are reduced from spectra and recorded in tristimulus XYZ values, CieLAB Lab* values, and other units. IODP Expedition 397T ICP-AES elemental analysis (solids)

Abstract Elemental contents in hard rock and sediment samples was measured by inductively coupled plasma - atomic emission spectrocopy (ICP-AES). Data are presented by element-wavelength pair (e.g., more than one calcium line may be reported). Elemental lines for which data do not exist for a particular expedition will not appear.
